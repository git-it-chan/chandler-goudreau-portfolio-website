Love’s Shadow

An Animated Short Film Feature

Project Overview

Type:
Animated Short
Brief:
Create an animated short based on voice-over audio
Tools:
AE, PS, AI, PR, Procreate, Reaper
Skills:
Animation, Rotoscoping, Music, Acting
Specs:
1920x1080, 24fps, 13m 11s
Date:
Dec.13.2023
Kinda' inspired by the novels from the Brontë sisters, this is a period-piece romance set in the 1800s—however, nothing is what it seems.

This was my most ambitious and unique submission, featuring two different animation styles. I wrote, directed, animated, acted, composed—everything. I drank way too many energy drinks for this one…

Pre-Production

I wrote two storyboards, one for animation and one for live-action blocking. I created animatics, recorded voices, and mapped out everything in advance.

Style & Design

Production

I stayed organized with 100+ lines of dialogue, color-coded tracks, and cue sheets. I filmed rotoscoping scenes four times for each character. Premiere was used for editing footage before animating.

Post-Production

Sound design, opening credits, color grading, grain, and blending animation with live action. This was the glue that tied everything together.

Reflections

Achievements: This piece pushed my technical and creative limits. I built complex rigs, composed a full soundtrack, and took my storytelling to the next level.

Challenges: Rotoscoping 6+ minutes nearly broke me. I sacrificed so much in my personal life for this passion project—but it was worth every frame.
