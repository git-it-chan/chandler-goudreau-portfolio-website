A VIKING STORY — Animated Short

A VIKING STORY

Type: Animated Short

Brief: Animate any word in a way that the animation reflects its meaning. I chose to make a war epic.

Duration: May 10–24, 2023 (2 weeks)

An epic tale of an army of Vikings setting off to battle and invade the King’s land.

Tools: After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator, Reaper

Skills: Animation, Illustration, Voice Acting, Music Composition, Sound Mixing

Specs: 1920x1080 · 24fps · 2m 29s · May 24, 2023

Originally, I was going to choose the word "twist" and have the letters dance with each other—but I ended up doing a full-on war epic short film with a much better word.

Achievements

Despite only having worked with After Effects for four months, I taught myself how to do walk cycles ahead of schedule. I took inspiration from Sigur Rós and wrote a song with gibberish lyrics that still sounded beautiful. The process included translating the synopsis into Icelandic and adapting it into musical rhythm.

Challenges

One scene had to be cut short due to time constraints. However, I took an even more creative approach to the setback, and that scene ended up being a class favourite. This taught me that time pressure can often lead to unexpected breakthroughs.
