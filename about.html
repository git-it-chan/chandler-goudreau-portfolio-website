ABOUT ME

The One Where I Explain My Origins (10/10 Friends Reference)

(motion) Graphic design is my passion

Graphic design has been a constant in my life without me even realizing it. From hand-drawing crappy Pokémon cards in grade 7 to the UI/UX design in games I developed to designing album covers—I've always been immersed in layout and visual storytelling.

When I'm not designing, I'm writing music. I currently sing in a synthpop/post-punk goth band, THE SEANCE . The amount of reception has been wildly unexpected because our music has been played in numerous radio stations and clubs all over the world. My solo music is more experimental and has me dabbling in A LOT of genres!

My background in filmmaking and acting adds a unique flair to my designs, teachers often picked up on this background due to the cinematic quality of my work. Drawing from my love for storytelling, I believe narratives are of upmost importance when you want to add depth and engagement to design.

Entering the Graphic Design program with initial intentions of web design, I unexpectedly fell in love with Motion Graphics. It's the perfect blend of all of my favourite skills – scoring, sound mixing, video editing, post-production, coding, and animation.
