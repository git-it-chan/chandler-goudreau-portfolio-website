ABOUT ME

(motion) Graphic design is my passion

From drawing crappy Pokémon cards in grade 7 to designing album covers and UI/UX in games—I’ve always been obsessed with visual storytelling. My background in filmmaking and acting adds a unique flair to my design process. Everything I create is rooted in narrative structure.

Outside of design, I’m also the vocalist for THE SEANCE, a synthpop/post-punk band. We’ve been played in clubs and radio stations around the world—and my solo music explores even weirder genres.

When I entered the Graphic Design program, I was set on becoming a web designer. Then I discovered Motion Graphics. It combines all my favorite things—music scoring, video editing, sound mixing, post-production, and animation. After Effects is both my best friend and worst enemy.
