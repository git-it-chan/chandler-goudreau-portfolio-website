ABOUT ME

(motion) Graphic design is my passion

From drawing crappy Pokémon cards in grade 7 to designing album covers to UI/UX in games I programmed—Graphic Design has always been a constant in my life. My background in filmmaking and acting adds a unique flair to my design process. Everything I create is rooted in narrative structure.

Outside of design, I sing in a post-punk/synthpop band, THE SEANCE. We’ve been played in clubs and radio stations around the world! My lesser known solo music project is more experimental and explores a wide-range of genres.

When I entered the Graphic Design program, I was originally set on Web Design. Then I discovered Motion Graphics. It combines all my favorite things—music scoring, video editing, sound mixing, post-production, and animation. After Effects is the coolest program—when it's not crashing...
