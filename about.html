ABOUT ME

The One Where I Explain My Origins (10/10 Friends Reference)

(motion) Graphic design is my passion

From drawing crappy Pokémon cards in grade 7 to the UI/UX design in games I developed to designing album covers—I've always been immersed in layout and visual storytelling. My background in filmmaking and acting adds a unique flair to my designs. Drawing from my love for storytelling, I believe narratives are of upmost importance when you want to add depth and engagement to design.

When I'm not designing, I'm always pursuing my biggest passion—music. I am the vocalist for a synthpop/post-punk goth band, THE SEANCE . We've had the unexpected thrill of hearing our songs played in radio stations and clubs all over the world! My solo music is more experimental and has me dabbling in just about every genre!

Entering the Graphic Design program with initial intentions of web design, I unexpectedly fell in love with Motion Graphics. It’s the perfect mix of everything I love–scoring, sound mixing, video editing, post-production, coding, and animation. Plus, After Effects is like the coolest program—when it's not crashing...
